INDIA TOP 5 TEMPLE TO VISIT

1ST PLACE

Badrinath Temple

Badarinath or Badarinarayana Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu, located in the town of Badrinath in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand, India.
Badrinath location

2ND PLACE

Dwarkadhish Temple

The Dwarkadhish Temple, also known as Jagat Mandir, is a prominent Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, located in Dwarka, Gujarat, India. It's one of the four Char Dhams (sacred pilgrimage sites) and a significant religious and cultural landmark. The temple is believed to have been built by Lord Krishna's great-grandson, Vajranabha, and is revered by Hindus as the place of worship for Dwarkadhish, or the King of Dwarka.
Dwarkadhish Temple location

3RD PLACE

Jagannath Temple

The Jagannath Temple in Puri, Odisha, is a prominent Hindu temple dedicated to the deity Jagannath, a form of Vishnu, and his siblings Balaram and Subhadra. It's a significant pilgrimage site in Odisha and one of the four Char Dhaams, the revered pilgrimage destinations for Hindus. The temple is known for its Kalinga architecture and is the site of the famous Ratha Yatra festival.
Jagannath Temple location

4TH PLACE

Ramanathaswamy Temple

Ramanathaswamy temple is a popular place of worship and prominent tourist attraction in Rameshwaram. The shrine is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the twelve jyotirlinga temples in India.
Ramanathaswamy Temple location

5TH PLACE

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

The Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, is renowned for its immense wealth, historical significance, and unique architectural style. The temple is also famous for its association with Lord Vishnu, its status as one of the 108 Divya Desams in Vaishnavism, and the various legends and stories surrounding its origins and mysteries.
